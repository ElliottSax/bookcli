### Hedging Words

### Empty Modifiers

## AUTO-FIX STRATEGIES

### Filter Words → Deep POV
- "She saw X" → "X happened/appeared"
- "He heard Y" → "Y sound description"
- "She felt cold" → "Cold bit through her jacket"
- "He noticed Z" → "Z specific description"

### Emotion Telling → Physical Showing
- Anger → Jaw clenched, fists tight, heat rising
- Fear → Cold sweat, rapid pulse, shallow breath
- Sadness → Throat tight, eyes burning, heaviness
- Joy → Lightness, warmth spreading, smile breaking through

### Purple Prose → Grounded Description
- "shivers down spine" → Specific physical reaction
- "heart hammered" → Choose ONE per book, vary rest
- "electricity at touch" → Warmth, texture, pressure
- "drowning in eyes" → Specific eye detail or moment
