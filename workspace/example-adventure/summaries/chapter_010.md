# Chapter 10 Summary

**Word Count**: 1,748

## Key Events
- Marcus enters shared consciousness - experiences merged awareness of three beings simultaneously
- Consciousness chaos: no boundaries, all thoughts shared, complete exposure
- Marcus learns full backstory through direct experience:
  - Lyara: Weaver world, scholar's daughter, talented in resonance magic
  - Thomas: Earth-born, descendant of refugees who crossed before Veil sealed
  - Met by accident through Veil, communicated for 5 years (palms pressed to barrier)
  - Friendship → fascination → love
  - Lyara found forbidden fusion crystal in father's collection
  - Modified it to work across Veil, thought love was enough
- Marcus experiences the fusion moment:
  - Physical fusion (bodies merged, bones/organs compressed)
  - Mental fusion (consciousness forced into single channel, identity dissolved)
  - Magical fusion (resonance channels braided, created corruption)
- Marcus identifies separation points (incomplete fusion in memories, hearts, magic)
- Realizes standard separation would take days (doesn't have time)
- Finds core solution: cut original binding thread at moment of fusion
- Manifests surgical blade, strikes violet thread three times
- Thread snaps, massive discharge begins
- Marcus uses own pattern as buffer to prevent violent death
- Guides separation gradually (consciousness → magical → physical)
- Successfully separates Lyara and Thomas, both alive and breathing
- Veil begins self-healing without corruption source
- Marcus collapses from extreme channeling damage
