## Chapter 9: The First Kiss (3,500 words)

**Key events**:
- Week three: Neck armor comes off (hardest piece yet, threads wrapped around spine)
- Catherine collapses from pain, Elara catches her
- Carries Catherine to bed in room above forge
- Catherine: "I thought I couldn't feel anymore. But this—you—"
- The kiss happens: Desperate, urgent, seven years of touch-starvation meeting three years of loneliness
- Not gentle. Not soft. Necessary.
- Both crying through it
- Pull apart, foreheads pressed together
- Catherine: "This changes everything."
- Elara: "Good."

**Progress**: Upper body completely free, only waist/legs/feet remain (30% left)

## Chapter 10: Learning Want (3,700 words)

**Key events**:
- Post-kiss: Both navigating new intimacy while continuing armor removal
- Catherine learning to want again: Small things (favorite food, warm bath, Elara's smile)
- Elara teaching Catherine how humans live: "You get to want things now. Practice."
- Montage of small moments: Sharing meals, Catherine laughing, casual touches
- First time Catherine sleeps: 4 hours in Elara's bed (Elara watches, counts her breaths)
- Catherine wakes panicked: "I haven't slept in seven years. I don't know how to not be vigilant."
- Elara: "Then I'll keep watch while you rest."

- Both aware deadline approaching: 2 weeks left

**Progress**: Waist armor removed, Catherine can breathe deeply for first time

## Chapter 11: The Warning (3,500 words)

**Key events**:
- Catherine's commander sends final warning: Return in 48 hours or be declared deserter
- Penalty for desertion: Execution
- Catherine: "Let them try."
- Reveals: She's been sending intelligence to Ferrum's Council
- Helping prevent war by sharing Argentum's battle plans
- Double treason now: Desertion + espionage
- If caught, both kingdoms want her dead
- Elara realizes: Catherine has chosen her completely, burned all bridges
- Fear and awe in equal measure
- Catherine: "I'd rather die free with you than live perfect and hollow for them."
- Elara: "You're not going to die."
- Catherine: "Promise?"
- Elara can't promise, kisses her instead

**Progress**: Legs exposed down to knee, feet still armored (20% remaining)

**Act 2A ends**: Established relationship, external pressure mounting, deadline approaching, armor removal entering final stage
