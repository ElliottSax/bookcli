**Part 1: The Before & After Map – Seeing the Transformation**

**Part 3: The Legacy Rep – Envisioning the Future with Confidence**

Set a timer for five minutes. Close your eyes. I want you to envision a project six months from now. Not a trivial one. Pick something that, from your old perspective, would have seemed daunting, a mountain. Maybe it’s proposing a new initiative at work, one that will face scrutiny.
