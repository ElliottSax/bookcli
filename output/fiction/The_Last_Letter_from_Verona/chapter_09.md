“This is my offer,” Chiara said, pulling her coat tighter, the moment of vulnerability passing like a shadow. “A public partnership. The library stays intact, here, under your care, but under the foundation’s banner. Or, I walk out that door, and the lawyers descend tomorrow. They will be less poetic than I am.”
