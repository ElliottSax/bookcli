# Scene & Story Structure

## SCENE-SEQUEL STRUCTURE (SWAIN)

### SCENE (Proactive Unit)
- **GOAL**: Clear, measurable objective
- **CONFLICT**: Opposition testing dedication
- **DISASTER**: Unanticipated outcome ("No, and..." or "Yes, but...")

### SEQUEL (Reactive Unit)
- **REACTION**: Emotional/visceral response
- **DILEMMA**: Choice faced because of disaster
- **DECISION**: What they decide next (becomes new Goal)

**Pacing Control**: Longer sequels = slower pace; compressed = faster

## MOTIVATION-REACTION UNITS (MRUs)

Reaction order (mirrors human experience):
1. **FEELING**: Immediate emotional response (involuntary)
2. **REFLEX**: Physical reaction (involuntary)
3. **RATIONAL**: Conscious action/speech

MOTIVATION always precedes REACTION.

## CHARACTER ARC FORMULA

**WANT** (external goal) vs **NEED** (internal requirement)

- **THE LIE**: False belief creating flaws
- **THE GHOST**: Past wound creating the Lie
- **THE TRUTH**: What they must accept to transform

### Arc Types
- **Positive**: Believes Lie → Confronts Truth → Transforms
- **Negative**: Believes Lie → Rejects Truth → Falls deeper
- **Flat**: Knows Truth → Changes world around them

## THREE-ACT STRUCTURE

### Act 1 (0-25%)
- Ordinary world
- Inciting incident (12%)
- Debate/resistance
- Break into Act 2 (25%)

### Act 2A (25-50%)
- New world/situation
- Fun and games (premise delivery)
- Rising action
- Midpoint: False high or false low (50%)

### Act 2B (50-75%)
- Bad guys close in
- Internal/external pressure
- All is lost moment
- Dark night of the soul
- Break into Act 3 (75%)

### Act 3 (75-100%)
- Gather resources
- Execute plan
- Climax (85-90%)
- Resolution
- New normal

## TENSION TYPES

### Macro (Novel Level)
- Story question
- Rising through acts
- Pinch points (37%, 62%)
- Stakes increase at breaks

### Micro (Page Level)
- Conflicting feelings
- Unexpected reactions
- Dialogue subtext
- Incongruent actions
- Dramatic irony

## HOOK PLACEMENT
- First line of chapter
- Last line of chapter
- Last line of scene
- Before flashbacks
- After major revelations
